About the Company

Medical Trust Hospital, a 750-bed multi-specialty acute-cum-critical care referral hospital, is one of the most well-equipped and premier hospitals in South India. Since inception in 1973, the hospital has grown with the commitment and passion of over 1,500 dedicated healthcare professionals, comprising internationally acclaimed doctors and efficient support staff.

We are known for our **personalized, patient-centered treatment**, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and high ethical standards. Our founder, **Dr. P. A. Verghese**, established a **world-class medical complex** that continues to serve international and local patients with equal passion.
